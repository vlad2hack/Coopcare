About CoopCare

CoopCare is a decentralized web application designed to solve issues in cooperative societies such as slow transactions, lack of transparency, and unorganized management. Built on the Solana blockchain, CoopCare aims to revolutionize how cooperative societies operate in Nigeria and beyond.
